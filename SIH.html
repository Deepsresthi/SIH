ABOUT OUR GAME

Life on this beautiful planet earth is short and sweet. To make this living more meaningful, its of utmost importance to understand the various roles of many indiviuals who are a part of our journey directly or indirectly. With an intention to achieve this aim on very interesting manner, we present our game "INDEBTED FOREVER". A game that equivalents to the roller coaster journey of life with insights.

CHILDHOOD
It emphasizes the beginning stage of life. The most beautiful life events that everyone would love to cherish. Additionally a fortune wheel spinner is present that lets the player to tap and try ones luck to gain gold coins.

RULES OF GAME

• Choose the player icon and number of player(Single player or Multiplayer).
• Roll the dice until go forward appears to land on the game board.
• Each cell represents the life events classified into 4 levels and set of 9 lifeline cards is made available.
• During the game the player can purchase the life line cards with the gold coins based on the player's requirements.
• Choosing the right life line card at each event, will increase the gold coins and permits the move to the next cell.
• If landed on the board cell that represents fortune wheel. Just spin the wheel and try your luck.
• For a single player minimum gold coins will be defined to qualify level 1 and move to level 2.
